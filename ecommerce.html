E-commerce

E-commerce refers to the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet. It involves the online transaction of products, digital services, and information, making it possible for businesses and consumers to conduct commerce electronically, without the need for physical stores or in-person interactions. E-commerce encompasses various activities, including :

  • Online shopping platforms
  • Supply chain and logistics
  • Digital marketing and promotion
